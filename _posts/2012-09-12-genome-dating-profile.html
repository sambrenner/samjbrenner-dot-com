The first assignment in Understanding Genomes was to create a dating profile for a randomly-assigned organism. I "have" Mycoplasma genitalium, an STD that is noteworthy for having the smallest known genome to constitute a cell.

I presented its dating profile in the form of a craigslist classified ad, which you can see here. I had originally wanted to model the syntax and grammar of the ad after what you would generally see on craigslist (click at your own risk), but that would have strongly hindered my ability to  communicate the information required for the assignment.
