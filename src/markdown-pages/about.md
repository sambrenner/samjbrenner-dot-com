Hello and thanks for visiting!

I am a software engineer with experience building educational, graphical, and creative tools for the web.

You can currently find me working at [Spotify](https://spotify.com). In the past, I've worked at the [Cooper Hewitt Museum](https://cooperhewitt.org), [Vidcode](https://vidcode.com), [CBRE Build](https://cbrebuild.com), and taught at [Parsons](https://www.newschool.edu/parsons/bfa-communication-design/). I am a graduate of the Rochester Insitute of Technology's New Media Design program (BFA, 2009) and New York University's Interactive Telecommunications Program (MPS, 2014).

Please write me! My email address is sam at samjbrenner.com.
